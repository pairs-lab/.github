# Perception Autonomy & Intelligent Robotics System Group (PAIRS)
 

[Perception Autonomy & Intelligent Robotics System (PAIRS) group](https://thanhnguyencanh.github.io/) at [Department of Robotics Engineering](https://fet.uet.vnu.edu.vn/bo-mon-ky-thuat-robot/), [Faculty of Electronics and Telecommunications](https://fet.uet.vnu.edu.vn/), [Vietnam National University - University of Engineering and Technology](https://uet.vnu.edu.vn/en) integrates various research disciplines required for design, implementation, experimental evaluation, and application of complex robotic systems. 
Research streams followed by PAIRS group members include motion and trajectory planning, SLAM, human-robot interaction, human-robot collaboration, control, communication and compression, and stabilization of ground, aerial, manipulation, humanoid, and modular robotics groups. 
From the application point of view, PAIRS deals with safety-critical systems, environment monitoring, self-stabilized convoys of ground and aerial robots, and search & rescue topics of modular robotics. 
Our team of Bc., Msc., and Ph.D. students and senior researchers cooperate to reach a common goal: deploying and applying robot systems in real-world challenging environments.

We welcome highly motivated Ph.D., Master's, and undergraduate students. If you're interested in our work and wish to join us, please submit your resume  to [Thanh Nguyen Canh](https://thanhnguyencanh.github.io/).
